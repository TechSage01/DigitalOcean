DigitalOcean offers grants of credits to open source projects to help with their development, infrastructure, and testing needs.

we want to give back to the open source ecosystem we
participate in and depend on

Grants are for credit towards DigitalOcean cloud computing services including virtual machines, cloud storage, managed databases, App Platform, and managed Kubernetes. Each grant is based on the individual needs of the project and which tier you apply for. Grants are awarded for one year. Applicants will need to re-apply each year if they want to renew their credit grant.

Any open source project with an OSI-approved license that meets our Grant Tier Criteria is eligible to apply.

Review the different types of OSI-approved licenses. If you haven't adopted one of these yet, you may not be quite ready for sponsorship. Consider researching your options. Here are two great resources for that: Understanding Open Source Software Licenses Guidance on Choosing a License

We will consider projects without a Code of Conduct however we highly encourage you to create one. Our friends at Indeed created this great resource on how to write your own: https://www.indeed.com/hire/c/info/write-a-code-of-conduct.
