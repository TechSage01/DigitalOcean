Compass Council

Share your thoughts, experiences, and ideas as
members of our Compass Council. We can't wait to
learn from you!

What you'll do

Whether you're participating in a study with our research team, meeting up with
your fellow Council members to share ideas, or offering your feedback in a
dedicated Slack channel, you'll have the chance to shape the future of
DigitalOcean's products and services.

Participate in user
research studies

As a Council member, you'll be first
on deck for research efforts focused
on learning more about your needs
and solving for your business.

Gather with your
fellow council
members

Join quarterly, hands-on sessions to
connect with DigitalOcean team
members, chat with your fellow
customers about your experiences,
and shape future products and
features.

Share your feedback
and ideas with us at
any time

As a Council member, you'll have
access to a dedicated Slack channel
where you can respond to quick
polls, chime in on discussion topics,
and share your feedback and ideas
in real-time.

You have questions; we have answers.

Have a question that isn't answered here? Drop us a line at

research@digitalocean.com

Any current DigitalOcean customer can request to join. Each study is unique. Participation expectations and details are described in invitations to specific studies, sent directly to you.

We will contact you directly via your preferred email if you've been selected for participation in a study. While we hope to meet everyone, we cannot guarantee study participation.

You may leave the council at any time. Simply email us to request to be removed from the list.

A few last details

Your personal information is important to us. We'll
keep your information private, and you can
unsubscribe at any time. You understand that the
information you provide will be used only for company
research purposes and will only be used in
accordance with DigitalOcean's Privacy Policy.

* To ensure participation, all members must adhere to DO's Community
Code of Conduct and Terms of Service and that DigitalOcean reserves
the right to remove user council members at any time for any reason.
